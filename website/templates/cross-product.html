Cross Product Calculator

To use the calculator, input the components of the vectors to be crossed. The resultant vector is listed at the bottom. Remember that the cross product is not commutative; which vector you enter first will influence the resultant vector.

Presently, only cartesian coordinates are supported as inputs; however, inputting vectors as a magnitude and a direction may be added in the future. The calculator only works in three dimensions. Support for seven-dimensional cross products is not planned.
